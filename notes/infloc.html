In the first half of this talk, I talk about localizations.

I. Localizations

Just like for categories, to localize an \[\infty-\]category is to declare

Definition. A functor \(f:\mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) of \(\infty\)-categories is a Joyal equivalence if it is an equivalence as a morphism in \(\text{Cat}_{\infty}\).
